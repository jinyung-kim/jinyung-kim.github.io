Jinyung Kim

Software Engineer / Researcher, Ph.D.

Brief Bio

While grounded in a strong foundation of static program analysis, various challenging yet exciting work experiences have drawn me to the industry. During my doctoral studies, I worked on multiple projects with Samsung Electronics, interned at Google, contributed to enhancing commercial static analyzers of Sparrow (then FasooSparrow), and worked part-time as a founding member at Deeping Source. After completing my Ph.D., I contributed to two startups: Deeping Source and Triple Corp., which later became InterparkTriple, and eventually Nol Universe. The problem-solving skills and research capabilities I developed during my studies enabled me to tackle complex challenges across diverse domains.

Since December 2024, I have come full circle and returned to my roots in program analysis with Sparrow, where I am starting a new chapter.
